Why uplighting?

Wireless & battery-powered

No cords across the floor and no heat. Our LED uplights run on battery, so we can place them cleanly and safely anywhere in your venue.

Warm-white to bold colour

RGBW lights matched precisely to your palette — from soft, candlelit warm-white tones to vibrant party colours, no candles needed.

Custom monograms & branding

Project your initials, logo or event branding in light onto the dance floor or a feature wall for a signature, photo-worthy touch.

Highlight your venue

Aim light to accent columns, doorways, décor and architectural features — far more than a flat wall wash.

Music-synced shows

On our Enterprise package, the lights pulse and change in sync with the music for a true dance-floor moment.

On-site technician

Our team sets up, programs and runs the lighting for you — included on the Professional & Enterprise packages.
